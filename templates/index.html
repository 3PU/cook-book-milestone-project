About VeggiePedia

Finding a large collection of vegetarian recipes without having to visit many websites can be quite a challenge these days.

This is why we created VeggiePedia. Similar to Wikipedia, the idea of VeggiePedia is to create a large database of vegetarian recipes over time with the help of its users.

Visitors can use VeggiePedia to browse the entire database of recipes and look for inspiration, but also add their own favorite recipes to the database.

To get started, click on 'Browse Recipes' in the top-menu to browse through the categories or click on 'Add Your Own Recipe' to add your own recipe to the database.
